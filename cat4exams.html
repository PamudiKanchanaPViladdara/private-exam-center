What is CAT-4 ?

The Cognitive Ability Test Fourth Edition (CAT-4) is a comprehensive assessment used by schools to understand students' learning preferences and academic potential across four key areas.

Verbal Reasoning

Tests ability to think with words and understand language concepts

Non-Verbal Reasoning

Measures spatial and visual pattern recognition abilities

Quantitative Reasoning

Assesses mathematical and numerical problem-solving skills

Spatial Ability

Tests ability to manipulate and understand 3D shapes and patterns

Practice Tips for Success

Our expert recommendations to help you perform your best on test day.

Before the Test:

Get plenty of rest the night before

Eat a healthy breakfast on test day

Practice with sample questions online

Arrive 15 minutes early to settle in

During the Test:

Read instructions carefully for each section

Work steadily - don't rush or spend too long on one question

Make educated guesses if you're unsure

Stay calm and focused throughout
