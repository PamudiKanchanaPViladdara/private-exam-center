A Summary of the Exam Registration Process

Once you have completed the exam registration form with details of the exams you wish to sit, we will register you for those units with the exam board. The exam board will then send us a Statement of Entry, confirming the exams (including any "cash-ins" for overall subject grades) you are registered for.

We will mail your Statement(s) of Entry along with a cover letter detailing the exam location and schedule.

On the day of your exams, you must bring your Statement(s) of Entry, a valid photo ID (passport or driving licence), and any required materials (e.g., a calculator for maths exams).

We receive electronic access to your exam results one day before the official release. On that day, we print and post your Statement of Results, ensuring it reaches you on the official release date. Along with your results, you will receive a cover letter outlining the available post-results services.

If you have requested overall subject grades ("cashing in"), your certificates will arrive a few months later and must be collected in person with a valid photo ID..
