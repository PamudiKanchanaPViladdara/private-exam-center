Understanding the Difference: GCSE vs IGCSE

GCSEs and International GCSEs (IGCSEs) are different qualifications:

  • GCSEs follow the UK Government curriculum and are assessed according to Ofqual regulations.
  • IGCSEs are designed by individual awarding bodies, each with their own content and assessment methods.

Both qualifications are highly respected. Our team can advise you on which option best fits your learning path.
