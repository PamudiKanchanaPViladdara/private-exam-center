Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance

How we collect, use, protect your personal information and comply with GDPR

Introduction

Private Exam Centre is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our services or visit our website, and how we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK data protection laws.

Last updated: 24/09/2025

Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Full name and contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Date of birth and nationality
  • Educational background and qualifications
  • Exam registration details and preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment providers)
Website Usage Data
  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

  • Processing exam registrations and bookings
  • Providing tuition and support services
  • Communicating important updates about your exams
  • Processing payments and maintaining financial records
  • Improving our services and website functionality
  • Sending marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Exam boards (CIE, Edexcel, AQA, OCR, WJEC) for registration purposes
  • Payment processors for secure transaction handling
  • Educational institutions when required for verification
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Service providers who help us operate our business (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Data Security & Protection Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Technical Safeguards
  • SSL encryption
  • Secure servers
  • Regular updates
  • Access controls
Organizational Measures
  • Staff training
  • Privacy policies
  • Data audits
  • Incident response
Third-Party Controls
  • DPA agreements
  • Security assessments
  • Regular monitoring
  • Compliance checks

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements. Exam records are typically kept for 7 years in accordance with educational regulations.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Essential cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.
