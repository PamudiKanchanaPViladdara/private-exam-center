Endorsement Details

Exam Centre offers the opportunity for candidates to sit GCSE practical exams for biology, chemistry and physics. We hold these sessions in professional laboratory facilities with highly qualified and competent science tutors to carry out these practical lessons.

There will be practice lessons where candidates will be taught how to accurately and confidently manage equipment and tutors will provide clear and concise instructions on how to conduct experiments safely and effectively.

Practice practical sessions will be led by one member of staff for every five candidates, giving you the opportunity to familiarise yourself with the lab and equipment while presenting any queries you may have.

Session Details

Structured approach to ensure complete understanding and competency

  • Prior to the sessions, the centre will conduct risk assessments to ensure that the labs and equipment are safe to use.
  • During the practical sessions, tutors will make candidates aware of health and safety hazards and ensure that candidates are made aware of how to correctly handle lab equipment. Tutors will then provide clear demonstrations of the experiments required for practical assessments.
  • This will follow with the opportunity for candidates to conduct both simple and complex experiments with the supervision of the tutors and practice experiments for the practical assessments. Throughout the sessions, tutors will be conversing with students to ensure there are no errors in the process.
  • The final session will allow candidates to demonstrate their learning and give tutors an opportunity to observe candidates and provide feedback on how well they have conducted the practical.
